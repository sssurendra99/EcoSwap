Frequently Asked Questions

üöÄ Getting Started

What is EcoSwap? +

EcoSwap is a sustainable e-commerce marketplace dedicated to eco-friendly products. We connect conscious buyers with verified sellers offering sustainable, environmentally responsible products. Every transaction on our platform contributes to reducing waste and promoting a circular economy.

How do I create an account? +

Creating an account is simple and free! Click on "Register" and choose whether you want to sign up as a Customer or Seller. Fill in your details, and you'll be ready to start shopping or selling sustainable products in minutes.

What's the difference between Customer and Seller accounts? +

Customer accounts allow you to browse and purchase eco-friendly products, track orders, and manage your shopping cart. Seller accounts enable you to list and sell your sustainable products, manage inventory, and track sales. You can always upgrade from a Customer to a Seller account later.

üõí Shopping

How do I purchase products? +

Browse our shop, select products you like, and click "Add to Cart". When you're ready to checkout, review your cart, enter your shipping information, choose a payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation and tracking information via email.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We currently accept cash on delivery (COD) and major credit/debit cards. We're continuously working to add more payment options including digital wallets and eco-friendly payment solutions.

Can I track my order? +

Yes! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also view your order status anytime by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section in your dashboard.

What is your return policy? +

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with a product, you can request a return within 30 days of delivery. The product must be unused and in its original packaging. Contact the seller or our support team to initiate a return.

üíº Selling on EcoSwap

How do I become a seller? +

Register for a Seller account by providing your business information and store details. Our admin team will review your application to ensure your products meet our sustainability standards. Once approved, you can start listing products immediately.

What products can I sell? +

You can sell any eco-friendly, sustainable products including:

  • Organic and natural products
  • Recycled or upcycled items
  • Zero-waste products
  • Renewable energy products
  • Eco-friendly fashion and accessories
  • Sustainable home and garden items

All products must meet our environmental standards and be verified by our team.

Are there any fees for selling? +

Creating a seller account and listing products is completely free! We only charge a small commission on successful sales to maintain and improve our platform. Detailed fee information will be provided during the seller registration process.

How do I manage my inventory? +

Your seller dashboard provides comprehensive tools to manage products, track inventory levels, update prices, and monitor stock. You'll receive automatic low-stock alerts and can easily add, edit, or deactivate products anytime.

üí¨ Account & Support

How do I reset my password? +

If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password securely. You can also change your password anytime from your profile settings.

How do I contact customer support? +

Our support team is here to help! You can reach us through the Contact Us page, email us directly, or use the live chat feature on our website. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

Can I delete my account? +
